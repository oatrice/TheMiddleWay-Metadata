### 1. Goal

As a practitioner, I want to see a visual representation of my spiritual progress and a clear checklist for the week, so that I feel motivated, calm, and know exactly what practices to focus on. This dashboard should serve as a welcoming and serene home page for my journey.

### 2. User Journey

1.  **Arrival:** The user opens the application and lands on the "Wisdom Garden" dashboard for the current week.
2.  **Observation:** The user immediately sees a visual representation of their progress (a growing tree or flower) and their total score for the week (e.g., "14/70").
3.  **Reviewing Tasks:** The user scrolls down to the "Daily Practices Checklist" to see what needs to be done. The list is organized by categories like "Giving" (‡∏ó‡∏≤‡∏ô) and "Ethics" (‡∏®‡∏µ‡∏•).
4.  **Action & Feedback:** The user identifies a practice they have just completed and taps its checkbox.
5.  **Instant Gratification:** The checkbox is marked as complete with a subtle, satisfying animation or sound. Simultaneously, the total score at the top of the screen increases, and the tree graphic grows slightly, providing immediate positive reinforcement.
6.  **Reflection:** The user decides to review a previous week's progress. They tap the "Week Selector" at the top and choose a past week number (e.g., "2").
7.  **Viewing History:** The entire dashboard updates to show the data for the selected week, including the final score, the state of the tree, and the completed checklist items for that period.

-   Notifications or reminders.
-   A profile or settings page.
